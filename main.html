My HomeTown


I have a very special place of My Hometown which is Khaga. It is situated between two beautiful rivers Ganga and Jamuna and also between two beautiful cities Allahabad and Kanpur. This is a small town known as the heart of fatehpur district. There are around 70 villages under its constituency.

Top five destinations to visits when you are in Khaga.

  1. Ayodhya Mandir
  2. Hanuman Mandir
  3. Station Park
  4. Sarafa bazaar
  5. Nahar

Must-try foods of khaga

  • Special Samosa from Sitaram
  • Cakes form Neha Sweets
  • Biryani
